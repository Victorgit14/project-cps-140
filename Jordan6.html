Pros

The Air Jordan 6 combines elements from seven different Jordan models, offering a durable, stable, and supportive build with good wear resistance. Some find the Air Jordan VI Retro to be comfortable, with its design contributing to its overall stability.

Description

The Air Jordan VI, released in February 1991, became iconic when Michael Jordan wore it to lead the Chicago Bulls to their first championship later that year. The shoe featured leather overlays with "2" and "3" to honor MJ's uniform number, a sculpted midsole, Vis-Air heel cushioning, and a translucent outsole. The Black/Infrared colorway also included rich suede. With a clean toe cap, lace locks, molded heel tab, and inner booty sleeve for comfort, the AJ VI became popular not only in basketball but also in pop culture, with comedian Jerry Seinfeld wearing it on his show. While wearing the AJ VI, Jordan averaged 31.5 points per game, earned his fifth straight scoring title, and was named Finals MVP, cementing his legacy.
