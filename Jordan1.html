Air Jordan 1

Release Date:1985

Price: $65.00 orignal retail price

The orignal designer is Peter Moore

Pros

The Air Jordan 1 is known for its iconic design, premium leather, and a variety of color options, making it a timeless and stylish shoe. Its popularity also helps it hold strong resale value, while its design provides good support and stability.

Description

The first Air Jordan sneakers came out in 1985 and started a big trend for cool shoes. Michael Jordan, a talented basketball player, was chosen by the Chicago Bulls and made a big deal with Nike, even though he wasn’t famous yet. Nike made the special shoes just for him, but the NBA didn’t like them because they didn’t follow the rules for team colors. Michael wore them anyway, and Nike paid the fine for him. He wore the shoes while winning an award for being the best new player that year! The shoes had a special Nike Swoosh logo and a cool Wings logo with a basketball and wings on it. They were made with tough leather and had holes in the toes to keep feet cool.

Cons

The Air Jordan 1 can feel stiff and heavy at first, and the soft leather may crease easily, especially around the toe. Some limited editions are expensive, and while designed for basketball, they may not be the best choice for high-impact activities.
